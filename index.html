About Me

Synthesizing Creativity and Technology

I am a multi-disciplinary creator with a passion for operating at the intersection of design, art, and technology. My journey has led me through the worlds of machine learning, user-centered design, 3D visualization, and audio engineering.

Whether I'm architecting a machine learning model, composing a musical score, or sculpting a 3D asset, my process is driven by a unified goal: to solve complex problems with elegant and engaging solutions. I believe the best innovations come from a fusion of analytical and creative thinking.
